OUR STORY

FROM A SINGLE UNIT TO A TRUSTED NATIONAL BRAND

EEPRON Plywood began as a single manufacturing unit under United Enterprises, built on a simple promise — deliver plywood that's flat, calibrated, and reliable, sheet after sheet. Over the years, that promise grew into two state-of-the-art factories, in Kolkata and Lucknow, producing full calibrated waterproof and non-waterproof plywood for furniture manufacturers, dealers, and construction companies across India.

Today, EEPRON stands for factory-direct pricing, consistent quality, and a supply chain that gets bulk consignments to your godown, on time, wherever you are in the country.
