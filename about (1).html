About EEPRON

A premium plywood manufacturing brand under United Enterprises, dedicated to delivering superior quality wood panel solutions across India since 2003.

Our Story

EEPRON Plywood was founded with a vision to revolutionize India's plywood manufacturing industry. Starting with our first manufacturing facility in Kolkata in 2003, we established ourselves as a trusted name in quality plywood production.

In 2011, we expanded our operations to Lucknow, doubling our production capacity and reaching a wider market across India. Today, EEPRON stands as a symbol of premium quality, reliability, and innovation in the plywood sector.

Our journey has been driven by a simple philosophy: deliver full calibrated, high-quality plywood directly to customers at factory prices. We eliminate middlemen, maintain strict quality control, and ensure on-time delivery to your godown.

With over two decades of experience, we continue to invest in state-of-the-art technology, skilled workforce training, and sustainable manufacturing practices to serve our customers better every single day.
